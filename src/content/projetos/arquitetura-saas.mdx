## Context

The product was evolving quickly on top of a tightly coupled monolithic codebase, creating deployment bottlenecks and scalability limits per business domain.

## Challenges

- Strong coupling between domains  
- Long deployment cycles  
- Limited scalability per module  

## Architectural approach

- Domain-Driven Design (DDD) and bounded context separation  
- Asynchronous, event-based communication between contexts  
- API Gateway as a single, cohesive entry point  
- Observability as a first-class architectural concern  

## Results

- Deployment cycle reduced by ~60%  
- Improved fault isolation and resilience  
- Independent scaling per domain  
- Higher operational predictability
