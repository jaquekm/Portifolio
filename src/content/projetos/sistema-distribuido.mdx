## Context

Critical application with global usage required a 99.9% SLA while expanding to new regions.

## Complexity

- Geographical replication  
- Global load balancing  
- Failover strategy and recovery time  

## Architecture

- Multi-region infrastructure  
- Active-passive database replication  
- Circuit breaker strategies and backoff  
- Distributed cache to reduce latency  

## Impact

- SLA elevated to 99.98%  
- ~80% reduction in downtime  
- Sustainable horizontal scalability
